### Objective
To demonstrate proficiency in Spark SQL by performing joins and descending aggregations on relational sports data within the Databricks environment.

## Activity 3: Linear Programming Optimization
### Objective
Solving a constrained optimization problem using Google OR-Tools (GLOP Solver) within a Databricks notebook.

### Problem Definition
**Maximize:** $Z = 3x + 4y$
**Constraints:**
1. $x + 2y \le 14$
2. $3x - y \ge 0$
3. $x - y \le 2$

### Results
* **Optimal Value:** 34.0
* **X:** 6.0 | **Y:** 4.0
